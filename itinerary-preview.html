Day 1 Arrival & South Goa Serenity
🌅
Morning
Fly in, check into Taj Exotica. Settle in, order breakfast by the infinity pool.
☀️
Afternoon
Head to Colva Beach — quieter than the north, perfect for a lazy December day.
🌙
Evening
Dinner at Martin's Corner — Goan prawn curry, feni cocktails, live music.
Day 2 North Goa & Nightlife
🌅
Morning
Explore Fort Aguada at 8am before the crowds. Views of the Arabian Sea are stunning.
☀️
Afternoon
Baga Beach for water sports — parasailing or a banana boat ride. Lunch at Britto's.
🌙
Evening
Sunset drinks at Tito's Lane, then dinner at Souza Lobo — legendary Goan fish thali.
Day 3 Spice Farms & Slow Goodbye
🌅
Morning
Spice farm tour at Sahakari Spice Farm — includes a full Goan lunch cooked on-site.
☀️
Afternoon
Browse the Anjuna Flea Market for souvenirs. Last dip at Anjuna Beach.
🌙
Evening
Early dinner at Pousada by the Beach. Head to airport or wrap up the trip.
💡 Travel Tips
December is peak season — book hotels at least 3 weeks in advance
Rent a scooter (₹400/day) — easiest way to move between beaches
Carry cash for beach shacks; most don't accept UPI
Water is warm (27°C) — great for swimming all 3 days
🏨 Where to Stay
Luxury Taj Exotica Resort & Spa, Benaulim — private beach, butler service, ~₹18,000/night
Mid The Postcard Cuelim — boutique heritage property, quieter location, ~₹8,000/night

Colva Beach
One of South Goa's quietest beaches. Perfect December weather — warm, breezy, and uncrowded.

Dinner at Martin's Corner
Legendary Goan restaurant. Try the prawn curry and butter garlic crab. Live music on weekends.

December is peak season — if you haven't booked dinner reservations, call ahead. Martin's Corner fills up fast.
